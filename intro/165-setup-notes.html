CS 165 F2F Course Setup Notes

This document contains a list of the steps to convert the online course to a Face to Face course.

Each semester, the course improvement department will change the due dates, etc for the next semester. Then, we can copy the reference course to be a F2F reference, make a few changes there, and then let each professor create their F2F section from that source.

  1. Copy the course to a F2F Reference section.
  2. Remove the following:
    1. Welcome - Syllabus
    2. Welcome - Schedule
    3. Welcome - Intro to Online Learning
    4. Welcome - Developers' Forum Instructions
  3. Add the following (From the F2F files sub-module):
    1. Welcome - Syllabus
    2. Welcome - Schedule
  4. Remove from the gradebook:
    1. Developer's forum assignments
    2. Final Exam
  5. Add to the gradebook:
    1. Exams category worth 15% of the final grade.
    2. 14 Teach : Team Final Exam Review - In the Team Activities category, worth: 160 points
    3. 15 Prove : Final Exam - In a the new exams category, worth 100% of that category.
  6. Adjust the weights of the gradebook to be as follows:
    1. Team Final Exam Review
    2. Final Exam
  7. Other things to think about:
    1. The instructor info on the "overview" landing page.
