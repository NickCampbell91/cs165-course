CS 165 Schedule

This course is organized at the week level. For assignments and due dates, there will be a consistent pattern every week.

A few things to note:

  • Items are due at 11:59pm on the days noted.
  • This schedule is subject to change at the instructor's discretion.
  • This course has been designed for the average student to earn an average grade by working 2 hours outside of class for every hour inside of class (as indicated by the approximate times shown in this table). For online students, this translates to 3 hours per credit, or 9 hours. Individual circumstances may require more/less time to be invested.
